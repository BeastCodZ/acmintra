Built for India's
financial safety

CashGuard is an open-source, privacy-first tool that helps you detect counterfeit currency and understand your financial health — entirely on your device.

🛡️

Counterfeit Detection

Upload a photo of any Indian banknote and get an instant AI verdict. The model checks watermarks, security threads, serial numbers, and more — all in under 2 seconds.

📊

Statement Analysis

Upload your Kotak, BHIM UPI, or bank PDF statement. We parse every transaction, categorise it, detect risk patterns, and give you a detailed financial breakdown.

💬

AI Financial Chat

Ask natural questions about your own spending data — "Who did I pay most?" or "Was there any suspicious activity?" — powered by a local AI model via Ollama.

🔍

Security Feature Map

An attention heatmap overlays exactly where the AI focused on your note — watermark, security thread, latent image, Gandhi portrait, and serial number panel.

📈

Health Score

A 0–100 score built from your spending ratio, risk flags, transaction patterns, and recurring payments. Know exactly where you stand financially.

⚡

Instant & Offline

The currency scanner runs entirely in the browser using ONNX Runtime Web — no internet needed after the first load. No data ever leaves your device.

Your data never leaves this device

Currency images are processed in-browser using WebAssembly — never uploaded to any server. Bank statements are analysed by a local Python server running on your own machine. No cloud. No tracking. No accounts.
